{% if request.session.lang == "en" %}About Saravali! {% else %}गावाची माहिती ! {% endif %}

Saravali village is located in Palghar Tehsil of Thane district in Maharashtra, India. It is situated 25km away from sub-district headquarter Palghar and 70km away from district headquarter Thane.The total geographical area of village is 256.16 hectares. Saravali has a total population of 673 peoples. There are about 163 houses in Saravali village. Palghar is nearest town to Saravali which is approximately 25km away.
