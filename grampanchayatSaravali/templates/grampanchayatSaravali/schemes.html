Saravali Grampanchayat Schemes

Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Yojana

As under this scheme the people who are disabled, disputed, blind, orphan children, divorced women, widow with a child, women who are freed from prostitution, people suffering from serious illness or disease, abandoned women, transgenders, outraged women etc . So all those people who comes under these categories will be included under this scheme. Moreover these people will get a financial assistance by the side of the Maharashtra state government.


Eligibility

  • The people who are disabled, disputed, orphan child, divorced and widow women, women freed from prostitution, outraged and abandoned women, people suffering from severe diseases etc will be included under this scheme.
  • In case of the disabled persons the disabilities should go beyond 40% in order to be a beneficiary of Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Yojana 2018.
  • To be the beneficiary of this scheme all the affected people must be below the age of 65 years.
  • The monthly family income of the person should not be more then Rs. 21000, if it goes beyond this amount then they will not be able to get the benefits of this scheme.
  • Most importantly to be a beneficiary of this scheme the person should be a permanent resident of Maharashtra state.


Benifits

  • The person who is affected individually will be provided with Rs. 600 per month.
  • In case the family has two or people affected than they will receive an amount of Rs. 900 per month .


Documents Required

  • Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Scheme Application Form
  • Age proof document
  • Income certificate to prove family income is below Rs.21000
  • Certificate of BPL (Below Poverty Line family).
  • Certificate issued by Talathi / Gramsevak Certifying deceased was Earning Head of the Family.
  • In case of major illness, Medical certificate need to be submitted. Medical certificate should be issued by the medical superintendent or the civil surgeon of Government hospitals.

PMJJBY is a renewal term insurance policy that, provides a yearly life insurance coverage of Rs. 2,00,000 in case of the demise of the insured person, at the most affordable premium rate of Rs. 330 per annum. In order to help you know more about the policy, we have discussed some of the key features and benefits offered by the policy.


Eligibility

  • Any person aged between 18- 50 years.
  • Requires saving bank account in any bank participating for this scheme.
  • Premium amount is Rs. 330 per annum.


Benifits

  • The total amount that can be claimed under the policy is INR 2 lakh only
  • Death caused by any reason your family we will get policy amount.


Documents Required

  • Copy of bank account to which policy is enrolled

Mazi Kanya Bhagyashree Yojna

This scheme belongs to Maharashtra State Government. This Scheme was launched to Increase Girl Child Birth rate, Level Up the Girl Education, Stop Sex Determination & Health in the State.


Eligibility

  • Girl born on 1st of August and onwards can benefit this scheme.
  • Mother with one or two girl child can benefit this scheme.
  • Mother with third child cannot benefit this scheme.
  • Beneficiary must be resident of Maharashtra to take advantage of this scheme.
  • Women in Child homes can also get benefited.
  • The family income with less than Rs 7.5 lakh will benefit from this scheme
  • Mother/Father should undergo family planning operation.


Benifits

  • Amount of Rs. 50000 for one girl child / Rs. 25000 for two girl child.
  • Point to be written.
  • Point to be written.
  • Rs. 100000 (one lakh) will be deposited on bank account once a girl is 18 years old.
  • GrandMother and GrandMother of Family with one girl child will get a gold coin.


Documents Required

  • Proof of operation after one or two girl.
  • Copy of Ration card
  • Copy of Adhaar card
  • Copy of Bank account's passbook
  • Birth certificate of child

Janani Suraksha Yojana

JananiSurakshaYojana (JSY) is a safe motherhood intervention under the National Health Mission. It is being implemented with the objective of reducing maternal and neonatal mortality by promoting institutional delivery among poor pregnant women.


Eligibility

  • All pregnant women under Below Poverty Line can avail this scheme.
  • All pregnant women of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.
  • Age of women while delivery should be 19.
  • This scheme is benefited till second child.


Benifits

  • Benefit of Rs. 600 if admitted in hospital.
  • Benefit of Rs. 1500 for cesarean delivery.

Documents Required

  • Copy of Bank account
  • Hospital admission card.
  • Copy of Adhaar card
  • Birth certificate of Beneficiary woman
  • Residence Certificate
